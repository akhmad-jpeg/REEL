## What is REEL?

REEL is an intelligent music downloader that combines the power of YouTube's vast audio library with Spotify's accurate metadata to give you perfectly tagged, high-quality music files.

---

## Why "REEL"?

The name REEL represents the concept of saving your music in an offline and orderly fasion sort of like cassette reels.

---

## Key Features

 **Smart Search** - Automatically finds the best audio version
 **Perfect Metadata** - Album art, track info, proper tags
 **Batch Downloads** - CSV, playlists, entire albums
 **Audio Only** - Filters out music videos
 **Detailed Logs** - Know exactly what failed and why

---

## Philosophy

REEL is built on three principles:

1. **Quality First** - No compromises on audio quality or metadata accuracy
2. **User Control** - You decide what, where, and how to download
3. **Transparency** - Clear logging, no hidden processes

## Quick Start

1. Install dependencies: `pip install -r requirements.txt`
2. Get free Spotify API credentials
3. Run: `python reel.py`
4. Configure in Settings (Option 7)
5. Start downloading!
